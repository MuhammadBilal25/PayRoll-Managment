About Payroll Management System

What is Payroll Management System?

A Payroll Management System is a software tool designed to organize all tasks of employee payment and the filing of employee taxes. These tasks can include keeping track of hours worked, calculating wages, withholding taxes and other deductions, printing and delivering checks, and paying employment taxes to the government.

Features of Our Payroll Management System

  • Employee Information Management
  • Payroll Processing and Calculation
  • Direct Deposit and Check Printing
  • Tax Filing and Reporting
  • Attendance Tracking
  • Customizable Reporting
  • Security and Compliance
